Our Menu

Hamburger

Our juicy, hand-crafted hamburger is made from 100% beef and served on a toasted bun. It's topped with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, tangy pickles, and your choice of cheese. Served with a side of crispy fries, this classic American favorite is sure to satisfy.

Pizza

Our delicious pizza is made with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and melty mozzarella cheese. Choose from a variety of toppings, including pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, and more. Perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying on your own.

Sushi

Our fresh sushi is made with the finest ingredients, including premium grade fish, sticky sushi rice, and crisp vegetables. Enjoy a variety of sushi rolls, including classic favorites like California and spicy tuna, as well as specialty rolls like the dragon roll and rainbow roll.
